## üé® Link Building Tactics

### 1. **Broken Link Building**
- Find broken links on pet-related sites
- Offer replacement content with link to Pawsitive Placements
- **Tools:** Ahrefs, Broken Link Checker

### 2. **Resource Page Link Building**
- Find "pet adoption resources" pages
- Request inclusion with valuable content
- **Target:** 50+ resource pages

### 3. **Skyscraper Technique**
- Create ultimate guide to pet adoption in BC
- Make it better than existing content
- Reach out to sites linking to competitors
- **Example:** "The Complete Guide to Pet Adoption in British Columbia 2025"

### 4. **HARO (Help a Reporter Out)**
- Sign up for HARO
- Respond to pet-related queries
- Get featured in articles with backlinks

### 5. **Partnership Link Building**
- Partner with local businesses
- Cross-link with complementary services
- **Examples:** Pet stores, veterinarians, groomers
